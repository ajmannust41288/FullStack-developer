The staff is courteous and attentive, providing assistance and guidance to shoppers as needed. The shopping center also organizes various promotional events, sales, and discounts throughout the year, offering visitors an opportunity to find great deals and bargains. Overall, Bilal Shopping Center in Islamabad, Pakistan, is a modern and well-equipped retail complex that caters to the diverse needs of shoppers.
